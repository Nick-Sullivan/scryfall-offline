Privacy Policy

Offline MTG Card Search — last updated July 2026

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No accounts. The app has no sign-up, login, or user profiles.
  • No analytics or tracking. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries of any kind.
  • Local data only. The card database, downloaded card images, your saved cards, and your filter preferences are stored only on your device. They are never transmitted anywhere and are deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Network use. The app connects to the internet only to download Magic: The Gathering card data and card images from Scryfall (scryfall.com / scryfall.io). These requests contain no personal information beyond what any web request technically includes (such as your IP address, visible to Scryfall as the server operator; see Scryfall's privacy policy).
